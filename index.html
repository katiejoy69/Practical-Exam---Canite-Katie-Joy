Available Jobs:

Programmer

Job Description: Responsible for writing, testing and updating digital codes.
Required Skills: excelled coding experience and standard communication skills such as active listening, attention to detail, problem solving and reading comprehension.
Educational Background: Degree in Computer Science or a related field
Potential Career Paths: Web Developer, App Developer, Computer Programmer
Average Salary: $78,368 per year

Data Analyst

Job Description: Collect, interpret and analyze data for large corporations. Help organizations make discoveries and form conclusions to help in decision-making processes.
Required Skills: data visualization, statistical programming, attention to detail, analytical thinking, problem solving, teamwork, mathematical skills
Educational Background: Bachelor’s Degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science or related fields
Potential Career Paths: Marketing Analyst, Business Intelligence, Systems Analysis, Data Analysis, Data Science
Average Salary: $74,377 per year

Cybersecurity Specialist

Job Description: Helps in maintaining the security of cyber networks. They implement security measures in networks, data centers and software systems. They install, monitor and upgrade malware and anti-virus protection software.
Required Skills: communication, collaboration, risk management, network security, adaptability, security audit, coding
Educational Background: Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
Potential Career Paths: Cybersecurity Administrator, Security Management, Penetration Tester, Ethical Hacker
Average Salary: $98,832 per year

Data Scientist

Job Description: Data scientists consider the human aspects of applying technology. This science involves collecting and analyzing data to solve everyday problems.
Required Skills: communication, data visualization, statistics and mathematics programming languages
Educational Background: Degrees in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, and Engineering
Potential Career Paths: Business Analyst, Statistics, Data Manager, Marketing Analyst
Average Salary: $55,173

Network Engineer

Job Description: They set up and design computer networks for a company. This may include configuring electronic or computer servers, activating equipment and properly connecting Wi-Fi services
Required Skills: design, implementation and the ability to monitor networks efficiently and effectively
Educational Background: Bachelor Degree in System Engineering or Computer Science
Potential Career Paths: Cyber Security, Network Manager, Freelance Consultant
Average Salary: $95,178
